Classic Lasagna

Home

Description

A layered Italian classic packed with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and stir in the marinara sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes until bubbling.
