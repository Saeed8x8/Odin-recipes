Easy Beef Tacos

Home

Description

Quick, crunchy, and savory beef tacos perfect for a fast weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 12 hard taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a pan, drain the fat, and add taco seasoning with water.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven for 5 minutes.
  3. Spoon the seasoned beef into the shells.
  4. Top with cheese, lettuce, and any extra toppings you want.
